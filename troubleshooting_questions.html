Common Troubleshooting Questions

Questions to Diagnose Common Issues

Here are some standard troubleshooting questions to ask users when diagnosing common tech support issues. These questions help identify potential causes and guide the troubleshooting process.

Hardware Issues

  • Is the device plugged in and powered on?
  • Have you tried restarting the device?
  • Are there any visible signs of damage, like cracks or broken components?
  • Are any cables loose, frayed, or unplugged?
  • When did the issue first start occurring?
  • Have you recently installed or moved any hardware components?

Software Issues

  • Have you tried restarting the application or software?
  • Are you using the latest version of the software?
  • Have you seen any error messages? If so, what do they say?
  • Did the issue start after an update or installation of a new program?
  • Have you tried reinstalling the software?
  • Are other applications affected, or is it only this one?

Network Connectivity Issues

  • Are you connected to Wi-Fi or using a wired connection?
  • Is anyone else experiencing similar connectivity issues?
  • Have you tried disconnecting and reconnecting to the network?
  • Is the network signal strong where you are working?
  • Have you restarted your router or modem?
  • Is there any scheduled maintenance or outage in your area?

Account and Login Issues

  • Are you sure you are entering the correct username and password?
  • Have you tried resetting your password?
  • Are you seeing any specific error messages when attempting to log in?
  • Have you recently changed your password?
  • Are you able to log in from a different device or browser?
  • Is there a possibility your account has been locked or suspended?

General Troubleshooting Questions

  • When did the issue first start?
  • Have there been any recent changes to your system or settings?
  • Have you tried restarting your computer or device?
  • Is the issue intermittent, or does it occur consistently?
  • Are other users experiencing similar issues?
  • What steps have you already tried to resolve the issue?
